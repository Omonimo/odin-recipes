Ribs

Description

Babyback pork ribs with black salt

Ingredients

    black salt
    babyback Ribs
    purple onion
    1 tooth of garlic
    black pepper
    olive oil

Steps

  • Cut the onion and garlic
  • mix the olive oil with, salt, black pepper,onion and garlic
  • marinate the ribs with the oil mix
  • wrap the ribs in aluminum and leave for a 24hrs in the fridge
  • preheat oven for 15 minutes
  • beak the robs in the over for 30 minutes with the aluminum
  • let the robs bake 15 min. without the foil
  • enjoy
